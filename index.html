The Services We Provide During Emergency Calls

When you reach out to us, you’re not just getting a tow truck—you’re getting a full-service team that understands the urgency and sensitivity of your situation. Here are some of the emergency towing services we offer in and around Orlando:

  • Accident Recovery: If you’ve been involved in a collision, we’ll safely tow your vehicle to a location of your choice or to a nearby repair facility. We work efficiently with local law enforcement and insurance companies to help minimize stress during an already difficult time.
  • Breakdown Towing: Mechanical failure? Dead battery? Overheating engine? We can safely transport your vehicle to your mechanic, dealership, or another service center without causing further damage.
  • Flat Tire Assistance: If you’re stuck with a flat and don’t have a spare, we can tow you to the nearest tire shop or assist with replacing the flat if you do.
  • Battery Jumpstarts: Sometimes a simple jumpstart is all it takes. We’ll assess your battery and, if needed, help you get to a parts store for a replacement.
  • Lockout Help: Locked your keys in your car? It happens to the best of us. Our team can help you regain access without damaging your vehicle.
  • Fuel Delivery: Ran out of gas on the way home? We’ll bring you enough fuel to get to the next gas station safely

We make sure every service is carried out with respect for your vehicle and your situation. Our trucks are clean, well-maintained, and equipped with the tools needed for safe loading and towing.

How to Prepare for an Emergency Towing Situation

While no one plans for car trouble, a little preparation can go a long way in making a stressful moment more manageable. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Save Our Number: Put (407) 860-8640 in your phone contacts under “Towing” so you can reach us instantly
  • Know Your Location: Use GPS to pinpoint your location or nearest intersection. It helps us get to you faster.
  • Stay Safe: If you’re on a busy road, stay inside your vehicle with your seatbelt fastened and hazard lights on until help arrives.
  • Keep Basic Supplies: A flashlight, phone charger, and water bottle can be incredibly helpful while waiting.

Having a plan—even a simple one—makes emergency towing less overwhelming. And when you know who to call, the experience becomes a lot less stressful.
